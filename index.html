How do I fix my golf slice permanently?
A golf slice is caused by an open clubface relative to the swing path at impact. The fastest fix: strengthen your grip by rotating both hands clockwise until you see 2.5 knuckles on your lead hand. This alone reduces most slices by 50%. Then check your clubface is square at address, and work on swinging from inside to out.

Read the complete slice guide →
How can a weekend golfer break 90?
Breaking 90 means 17 over par — roughly bogey golf. The fastest path: eliminate blow-up holes (triples and worse), improve your short game (60% of all shots happen inside 100 yards), and always aim at the center of greens rather than tucked pins. You can make 3–4 double bogeys and still shoot 89.
