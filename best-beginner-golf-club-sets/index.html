What Beginners Actually Need

Cavity-back irons, a hybrid instead of 3 and 4 irons, a high-lofted fairway wood, a mallet putter, and a stand bag. You do NOT need 14 clubs, blades, or anything over $600.

What to Skip Completely

Avoid sets under $150 — shafts are too heavy and inconsistent. Skip blade irons until you break 90. Skip 1, 2, and 3 irons completely.

Frequently Asked Questions

What golf clubs should a complete beginner buy?
A complete beginner should start with a full set package: driver, 3-wood, 4-hybrid, 5–9 irons, pitching wedge, and putter. The Wilson Profile SGI (~$299) or Callaway Strata (~$249) are the best options.
How much should a beginner spend on golf clubs?
$200–$400 for a complete club set. If budget is tight, the Callaway Strata at $249 is excellent. If you can stretch to $350–$400, the Wilson Profile SGI is a step up.
