Why Cubical Golfer Exists

Most golf content is written for either beginners who just picked up a club, or serious low-handicappers with unlimited time and money. There's almost nothing for the golfer in the middle — someone with a 12–22 handicap, a full-time job, maybe a family, who plays 15–25 rounds a year and genuinely wants to get better.

That's who Cubical Golfer is for. Every review, guide, and tutorial is written from the perspective of a golfer who has limited time to practice, a real budget, and high expectations for the Saturday round.

We test gear ourselves — purchased with our own money, tested over real rounds at real courses. No manufacturer loans. No sponsored coverage. When we say something is the best, it's because we've hit shots with it and compared it against the alternatives.

Our Approach

Every piece of content on this site is built around one question: does this actually help a weekend golfer play better or enjoy golf more? If the answer is no, we don't write it.

We use affiliate links — mostly Amazon, PGA Superstore, Golf Galaxy, and manufacturer direct. When you buy through our links, we earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. That's what keeps the site running and the reviews honest.
