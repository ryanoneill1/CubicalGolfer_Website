Safety first

Always ensure your net is rated for driver-speed balls before hitting full shots. The Callaway Tri-Ball net and similar targets are for chip and pitch practice only — not full swings. Net failure during a full swing is a serious safety hazard.

Frequently Asked Questions

What size net do I need for a driver?

For driver shots with any margin for off-centre hits, aim for at least 10 feet wide × 7 feet tall. The Rukket and Net Return both meet this requirement. Narrower nets risk ball escapement on heel or toe shots.

Can I hit full driver shots into a home net?

Yes, provided the net is rated for full-swing use. Never use a chipping net or target net for full driver shots. The Rukket Haack and Net Return Pro are both specifically built for full-swing use, including driver.

Final Recommendation

The ideal setup depends on your budget and goals. Start with the Rukket Haack Net and a Dura-Pro mat if you're not sure how often you'll use it. If you're committed to regular practice, invest in the Net Return Pro and Fiberbuilt mat — the ball return feature and joint-safe mat construction will make you actually use the setup.
