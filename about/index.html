Built for the Office Golfer

Cubical Golfer is an independent golf media site for everyday players who squeeze rounds in on weekends, obsess over their handicap on Monday, and refuse to stop getting better.

Who We Are

We're weekend golfers with day jobs. We play 25–40 rounds a year on Saturday mornings and occasional twilight rounds after work. We don't have tour coaches or sponsor budgets. We have the same $300 to spend on gear that you do, and we want to know exactly what's worth it.

Every piece of gear we recommend was purchased with our own money and tested over multiple real rounds — not on a demo day, not in a manufacturer's testing facility. On actual golf courses, in actual conditions, by actual 10–20 handicappers.

Our Editorial Policy

We earn affiliate commissions from some links on this site. This means if you click a link and buy something, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This does NOT affect our rankings or recommendations. We have declined to recommend products we were paid to promote because they weren't genuinely the best option.

We never accept manufacturer loans for reviews. Every product we test is purchased by us. No manufacturer has editorial control or advance review of our content.

Our Testing Process

🏌️

Tested Over Real Rounds

Every piece of gear is used over a minimum of 5–10 rounds before we write about it. We don't form opinions from spec sheets.

💰

Independently Purchased

All products are purchased by us. No freebies, no manufacturer loans, no sponsored content hidden as reviews.

⛳

Written for Weekend Golfers

Our target reader is a 12–22 handicapper with limited practice time and a real budget. We write for that person, not for tour players.

🔄

Regularly Updated

Every guide is reviewed and updated at minimum annually. Gear that no longer represents best value is removed.
