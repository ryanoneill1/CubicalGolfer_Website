Spikeless vs Spiked — Which Is Better for Walkers?

Spikeless shoes are lighter and more comfortable for long walks. Spiked shoes give more traction on wet grass and steep slopes. For most weekend walkers on standard parkland courses, spikeless is more comfortable. For hilly or wet courses, spiked is better.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the most comfortable golf shoe for walking?
The ECCO Biom C4 ($220) has the most comfortable outsole for long rounds. For value, the FootJoy Flex XP ($120) is the most comfortable spikeless shoe under $150. Both require zero break-in.
How long do golf shoes last?
Premium leather shoes (ECCO, FootJoy) last 3-5 years. Synthetic shoes last 1-2 years. Spikes wear down after 30-50 rounds on abrasive paths.
