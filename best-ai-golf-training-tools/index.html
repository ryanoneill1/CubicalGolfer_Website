Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best home golf simulator under $3,000?
The SkyTrak+ (~$2,995) is the best home golf simulator under $3,000. Accurate launch monitor data integrating with WGT Golf, E6 Connect, and The Golf Club simulation.
Can AI golf apps actually improve your swing?
Yes — for identifying specific, repeatable faults. AI apps are excellent at spotting grip issues, over-the-top moves, and early extension. Think of them as a starting diagnosis, not a replacement for an instructor.
