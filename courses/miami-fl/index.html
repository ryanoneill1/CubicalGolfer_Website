🟢 Public Golf Courses in Miami

Crandon Golf at Key Biscayne

🏌️ public 💰 $55–$100 ⛳ 18 holes 📊 Rating: 72.9

Former host of the PGA Tour's Honda Classic. Stunning bayfront setting on Key Biscayne. One of the best public courses in South Florida.

Palmetto Golf Course

🏌️ public 💰 $35–$65 ⛳ 18 holes 📊 Rating: 70.5

Miami-Dade County's best municipal course. Good conditions and excellent value.

⭐ Resort Golf Near Miami

Trump National Doral — Blue Monster

🏌️ resort 💰 $150–$300 ⛳ 18 holes 📊 Rating: 74.6

Former home of the WGC-Cadillac Championship. The Blue Monster is legendary. Resort stay gives best tee time access.

Don Shula's Hotel & Golf Club

🏌️ resort 💰 $60–$110 ⛳ 18 holes 📊 Rating: 70.8

Named after the legendary Miami Dolphins coach. Good conditions and reasonable rates for the area.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best public golf courses in Miami?
The best public golf courses in Miami include: Crandon Golf at Key Biscayne, Palmetto Golf Course. All offer excellent conditions and value for weekend golfers.
What is the best time of year to play golf in Miami?
The best golf season in Miami is November through April (summer is very hot and humid). Plan tee times in advance during peak months — popular courses book up 2–4 weeks ahead.
How much does it cost to play golf in Miami?
Public golf in Miami ranges from $20–$200 depending on the course and tee time. Municipal courses run $20–$55. Semi-private courses $60–$150. Twilight rates after 3pm can cut peak pricing by 30–50%.
