✅ Local Golf Guide

Houston has abundant public golf at excellent prices. The flat terrain means fast rounds, and the warm climate makes October through May the prime season. Some of the best-value public golf in the South is within 30 minutes of downtown.

🟢 Public Golf Courses in Houston

Hermann Park Golf Course

🏌️ public 💰 $20–$35 ⛳ 18 holes 📊 Rating: 68.4

Classic parkland layout in the museum district. Great value for a quick round.

Memorial Park Golf Course

🏌️ public 💰 $40–$70 ⛳ 18 holes 📊 Rating: 72.1

Completely renovated in 2020. Former home of the Houston Open. Now one of the best public courses in Texas.

Wildcat Golf Club

🏌️ public 💰 $45–$80 ⛳ 36 holes 📊 Rating: 71.8

36 holes in southwest Houston. Excellent conditions and challenging layout at a fair price.

Tour 18 Houston

🏌️ public 💰 $60–$100 ⛳ 18 holes 📊 Rating: 72.6

Holes modeled after famous tour venues including Augusta National, Pebble Beach, and more. Unique experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best public golf courses in Houston?
The best public golf courses in Houston include: Hermann Park Golf Course, Memorial Park Golf Course, Wildcat Golf Club. All offer excellent conditions and value for weekend golfers.
What is the best time of year to play golf in Houston?
The best golf season in Houston is October through May. Plan tee times in advance during peak months — popular courses book up 2–4 weeks ahead.
How much does it cost to play golf in Houston?
Public golf in Houston ranges from $20–$200 depending on the course and tee time. Municipal courses run $20–$55. Semi-private courses $60–$150. Twilight rates after 3pm can cut peak pricing by 30–50%.
